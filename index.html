03 / RECENT PROJECTS

Built by Spectranet

A glimpse into what we've engineered — real products solving real problems across education and beyond.

AI · EDTECH

Atlas AI

SCHOOL INTELLIGENCE PLATFORM

Atlas AI is an AI-powered school management platform that unifies administrators, teachers, and parents on a single intelligent ecosystem. Teachers record grades, attendance, and academic honours in real time; administrators gain full visibility into teacher activity and student performance across every class; and parents receive a live window into their child's academic journey. The platform also handles complete fee management — from creating class-specific fee structures to tracking individual payment status — making Atlas AI the all-in-one command centre for the modern school.

AI-Driven Multi-Role Fee Mgmt Analytics
OFFLINE · EDTECH

Offline Edu App

SMART ASSESSMENT ENGINE

A fully offline assessment platform built for classrooms with zero internet dependency. Teachers craft rich, multi-format assessments — objective, theory, and essay — for classwork, tests, and exams, then export them directly to students' laptops. Students complete and submit assessments within the app, which intelligently auto-grades responses, displays instant scores, and compiles full result sheets ready for printing or digital distribution to parents. No Wi-Fi. No disruptions. Just seamless, smart assessment.

100% Offline Auto-Grading Multi-Format Exportable
OFFLINE · ADMIN

PetraEduApp

OFFLINE SCHOOL MANAGEMENT SUITE

PetraEduApp takes everything the Offline Edu App does and elevates it into a full school management suite — all without ever needing an internet connection. It retains the complete offline assessment engine for teachers and students while introducing a dedicated admin layer that gives school administrators real-time oversight of teacher activity, student performance, and academic progress across the institution. Three roles, one powerful offline platform — purpose-built for schools that refuse to let infrastructure limit education.

Fully Offline Admin Dashboard 3-Role System Smart Grading
